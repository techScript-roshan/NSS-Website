Satyamev Jayate

About the event

The National Service Scheme (NSS) organized a remarkable event titled "Satyamev Jayate: Change is Possible" on September 11, 2023, held at the Mechanical Seminar hall from 2:30 p.m. to 4:00 p.m. The primary objective of this event was to create a positive impact on society and serve as a platform for students at Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College to engage in discussions concerning pressing social issues.
The event commenced by encouraging participants to share their thoughts, ideas, and potential solutions regarding critical social matters in an interactive session that allowed students to freely express their opinions and concerns. Focused on raising awareness about various social challenges, the event sparked in-depth discussions aimed at exploring tangible steps to address real-world problems. The goal was to equip participants with knowledge and motivation, inspiring them to take meaningful action and actively contribute to positive change within the college community.
An integral part of the event was the showcasing of videos on various social topics, offering valuable insights into the challenges faced by society. These videos served as a foundation for the subsequent discussions. Students were then divided into teams, each given time to prepare their perspectives on a specific social topic, allowing for a deeper exploration of the issues at hand. The event concluded with a presentation session, where one member from each team presented their point of view on the chosen topic. This segment encouraged constructive dialogue and the exchange of diverse viewpoints, contributing to a comprehensive and impactful event.
