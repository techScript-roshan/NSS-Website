Drug Awareness Campaign at School

About the event

The National Service Scheme (NSS) organized a school visit to the government school in Doraha, constituting an educational trip for students. Conducted on October 7, 2023, the purpose of this visit was to promote awareness among students and foster educational engagement. The chief guests for the event were Harvinder Roop Rai, the Principal of the government school in Doraha, and Dr. Sehijpal Singh, the Principal of GNDEC.
The event featured a skit performance with a dual focus on educating attendees about drug addiction and peer pressure. The program commenced with an inaugural speech that extended a warm welcome to Principal Sehijpal Singh Roop of GNDEC and Principal Harvinder Roop Rai of the government school in Doraha. The skit on peer pressure was presented by 1st-year engineering students, delivering a powerful message about how peer pressure can negatively impact the social and emotional development of students. Another skit on drug addiction was performed by 1st-year engineering students, covering serious topics such as the adverse effects of drugs on a person's health and the detrimental impact on the happiness of various families. The event successfully combined education and performance, contributing to a holistic and impactful experience for all participants.
